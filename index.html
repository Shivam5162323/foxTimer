Privacy Policy for FoxTimer

Last Updated: September 3, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to FoxTimer, a focus timer application that helps you track study sessions, breaks, and improve productivity. FoxTimer operates with a fundamental privacy-first approach: all your data remains exclusively on your device.

2. Information We Collect

No Personal Data Collection

FoxTimer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data created within the app—including timer settings, focus sessions, break tracking, and preferences—resides solely on your device.

Local-Only Data

The following information is stored locally on your device and never leaves it:

  • Timer settings and configurations
  • Focus session history and statistics
  • Break tracking data
  • Theme preferences and clock font selections
  • Focus mode settings

3. How We Use Your Information

Since we don't collect any data, there is no usage of personal information. All functionality happens locally on your device:

  • Tracking focus sessions and breaks
  • Displaying productivity statistics
  • Applying your selected themes and fonts
  • Enabling focus mode functionality

4. Data Storage and Security

All your data is stored locally on your device using Android's secure local storage. We don't use external servers or cloud storage for any user data. The security of your data depends on your device's security measures.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We Do Not Share Any Data

FoxTimer does not share, sell, trade, or transmit any user data to third parties—because we don't collect any data in the first place. Your focus patterns and productivity statistics remain private on your device.

6. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access your data directly within the app
  • Modify or delete your data through the app's settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data

7. Third-Party Services

FoxTimer does not integrate with any third-party services that would require data sharing. The app functions independently without external dependencies.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If we update this privacy policy in the future, we will post the changes within the app and update the "Last Updated" date. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically.
