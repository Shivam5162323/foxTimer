What is FoxTimer?

FoxTimer is an intuitive focus timer application built for students, professionals, and anyone looking to improve their productivity. Using proven time management techniques, FoxTimer helps you structure your work and break sessions for optimal focus and recovery.

About the Developer

Independent App Developer

FoxTimer was created by a solo developer passionate about productivity tools and user privacy. The app was born from personal frustration with existing timers that either lacked features or compromised user privacy.

Our mission is to provide a clean, effective focus timer that respects your data and helps you achieve your goals without tracking your every move.
